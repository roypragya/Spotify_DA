Spotify is a portmanteau of "spot" and "identify".
It is one of the largest music streaming service providers.
It has over 433 million monthly active users, including 188 million paying subscribers, as of June 2022.
Spotify is currently available in 180+ countries.
Total mobile app downloads for Spotify was 28m, including 6m iOS app downloads and 22m Android app downloads.
There are over 80 million tracks, including more than 4 million podcast titles available on Spotify
